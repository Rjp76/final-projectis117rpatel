Celty Sturluson
Headless horsewomen looking for her head in Ikebukuro, Tokyo, Japan.

Shinra Kishitani: Underground doctor, roomate to Celty.

Izaya Orihara: Information Broker based in Shinjuku.

Mikado Ryuugamine: High school student, leader of the colorless online gang, the Dollars.

Masaomi Kida: High school student leaer of the color gang, the Yellow Scarves.

Anri Sonohara: High school student. Wields a possessed sword calle 'Saika'.

Shizuo Heiwajima: Strongest Man in Ikebukuro, and can lift vending machines from the ground. has a known rivalry with Izaya.

Kyohei Kadota: Member of the Dollars, tough street fighter. 'Leader' of his group of friends.

Erika Karisawa: Part of the otaku torture technician duo, part of Kadota's gang.

Walker Yumasaki: Part of the otaku torture technician duo, part of Kadota's gang.

Saburo Togusa: Driver for Kadota's gang. Has known Kadota longer than the other two, and was once a respected fighter.
